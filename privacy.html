Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

This policy explains what happens to the details you share with us through this website.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information we collect

When you submit the lead form on this site, we collect the information you provide, which may include your name, phone/WhatsApp number, business type, budget range, and any message you enter. We do not collect payment information through this website.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and discuss your advertising needs
  • To prepare and share a free ads audit or proposal
  • To contact you via phone, WhatsApp, or email regarding your enquiry
  • To maintain internal records of enquiries and leads

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. How your information is stored

Form submissions are recorded in a private, access-controlled Google Sheet and trigger an internal email notification. Access is limited to Harini Shankar and anyone directly assisting with client enquiries.

4. Cookies and analytics

This site may use basic analytics to understand traffic and improve the browsing experience. No sensitive personal data is collected through analytics beyond standard, non-identifying usage patterns.

5. Third-party services

We may use third-party tools such as Google Sheets, Gmail, and WhatsApp to manage and respond to enquiries. These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the information you've shared with us at any time by contacting us using the details below.

7. Data retention

We retain enquiry information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and maintain business records, after which it may be archived or deleted.

8. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, reach out at 89785 40150 or via WhatsApp.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
